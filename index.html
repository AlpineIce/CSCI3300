Poker Rules

  • Each player is dealt two cards per hand face down
  • Players take turns betting-they can also fold, call, raise, or check
  • Comuunity cards A.K.A the river, is revealed in stages: flop(3 cards),turn(1 card), and the river(1 card)
  • The best five-card hand wins. (view the poker hand table in game to see the rankings from highest-lowest: top to bottom
  • At the end of the round, the betting stops and each player reveals their hand.
